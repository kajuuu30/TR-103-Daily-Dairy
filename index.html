About Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College (GNDEC)

GNDEC, established in 1956, is one of the oldest premier engineering institutes in northern India, offering diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Renowned for its academic excellence, the college emphasizes practical learning and industry collaboration. Its commitment to innovation prepares students for successful careers in engineering.

About CSIR-CMERI

CSIR-CMERI, a leading R&D institution in India, focuses on advanced technologies in mechanical engineering and is instrumental in innovation. Its Ludhiana branch specializes in post-harvest technology and farm machinery development, addressing agricultural challenges through research and development. The institute plays a key role in enhancing productivity and sustainability in the agricultural sector.
